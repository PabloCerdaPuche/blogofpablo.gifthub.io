Spaghetti

  • Time= 1 hour
  • Serves= 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti packet 500 grams
Water 500 ml
Salt 1 tablespoon
Butter 50 grams

Step 1: Boil the water in a vessel with high walls

Step 2: Add salt and the raw noodles while the water is boiling.

Step 3: Let the noodles cook till they are aldente.

Step 4: When the noodles are ready, wash them in clean water, after removing the hot water.

Step 5: Put the noodles back inside the vessel. Add the butter so they don't stick.

Step 6: Enjoy the Spaghetti with favourable toppings.

Strawberries

  • Time: 5 months
  • Serves: 20 people
Ingredients Quantity
Strawberry seeds 1 packet
Water 100 litres approx.
Earth 2 square metres

Step 1: Till the Earth.

Step 2: Add the strawberry seeds to the earth.

Step 3: From that momemt on, sprinkle 1 litre of water everyday.

Step 4: Wait.

Step 5: When the strawberries are the size of nuts, harvest them.

Step 6: Wash them and eat them with whipped cream.
